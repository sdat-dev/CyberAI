Dear Colleagues,

Welcome to the new digital home for the Cybersecurity & Artificial Intelligence (AI) Institute, which highlights the breadth of cyber and AI strengths across the University at Albany academic and research enterprise.

The world that we live in is becoming increasingly digital, as we use the internet for communication, commerce, education, and entertainment. As more and more of our daily lives are being conducted online, we are putting our personal and secure information in danger. Over 2015-2019, the Internet Crime Complaint Center (IC3) received a total of 1,707,618 complaints, reporting a loss of $10.2 billion [Source]. Moreover, as e-commerce and online banking increase in popularity, there will also be an increased number of transactions that expose personal and financial information to hackers.

AI is a transformative technology, which is in the midst of creating another industrial revolution. AI will indeed be instrumental in cybersecurity and in the prevention of malicious cyberattacks as we continue to advance the enabling of machines to learn and make decisions similar to that of humans. Over the next decade, novel AI approaches will be developed and employed to identify potential cyber threats and security issues, and will be critical in our building the cyberdefense necessary to prevent attacks before they happen.

The University at Albany is ready to meet this challenge and opportunity. With the establishment of the Cybersecurity and AI Institute, the University brings together over 120 faculty and researchers from across 6 colleges and schools and 5 research centers and labs, to become the go-to place for Cybersecurity and Artificial Intelligence education and research.

For students, you will be able to visit the Institute’s website to:

  • Over 200 course offerings in the University’s Cyber and AI degree programs
  • Compare undergraduate and graduate academic programs
  • Identify a faculty member or researcher with a particular expertise in the Cyber/AI field for advisement and mentorship

For faculty, researchers, or experts/leaders interested in pursuing collaborative research and/or extramural funding, you will be able to:

  • Identify a faculty scholar or researcher in a particular discipline
  • Build a team of investigators to pursue federal funding
  • Leverage existing program, research center/lab or university-wide designations
  • Learn about extramural funding programs and opportunities to pursue
  • Build a public-private partnership with a university, business and/or government agency

Across the University, our faculty, researchers and students are already making a difference in addressing one of our nation’s most critical challenges. Within the Cybersecurity and AI Institute’s academic programs spanning the University, we are educating and training the next generation, advancing state of the art research and development, and pursuing public and private sector partnerships to address today’s problems with tomorrow’s solutions.

We encourage you to join us at the Cybersecurity and AI Institute at the University at Albany as we continue our on-going work in preparing for a safer and brighter future.

Sincerely,

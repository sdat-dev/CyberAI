Inaugural: Lightning Talks on Artificial Intelligence

Time: 2:00 PM - 4:30 PM, April 7
Location: Standish Room, Science Library, Uptown Campus, University at Albany

All faculty scholars, researchers, postdoctoral associates, and students working in AI or related fields, or just interested in learning about the ongoing AI research are cordially invited to join our speakers and learn about their projects and explore the possibility of starting new collaborative projects. Each speaker will provide an overview of their research followed by ample time for Q&A. Refreshments will be provided.

Artificial Intelligence plays an important role in our lives and is expected to rapidly grow on a global scale and permeate almost all aspects of life, businesses, society, and national defense. The Federal government and NY state have put Artificial Intelligence at a high priority and are allocating funds for research and development projects through different agencies. Industries across the country are participating in the AI revolution and are looking for research and development partners to get ahead of their competitors.

This inaugural session of Lightning Talks on Artificial Intelligence is a collaborative venture between the University at Albany and SUNY Polytechnic Institute. It is jointly sponsored by the Division of Research and the Office of the Provost at UAlbany.

We look forward to your enthusiastic participation in this event. Please feel free to invite colleagues and students who might be interested or benefit from the presentations at this session.

For more information or to volunteer to speak at future Lightning Talks, please contact satyenkumar@albany.edu
